Overview

This document outlines key use cases for the Enterprise Integration Platform. Each use case demonstrates how the platform automates workflows, integrates systems, and provides unified visibility across the organization.

New Customer Onboarding
UC-001

Actors

Sales Team Engineering Team Support Team Finance Team

Systems Involved

HubSpot Jira Zendesk ERP Intuit Enterprise

Description

When a sales deal closes in HubSpot, the platform automatically orchestrates the complete customer onboarding process across all systems, creating projects, accounts, and initiating billing without manual intervention.

Workflow Steps

  • Sales rep marks deal as "Closed Won" in HubSpot
  • Workflow Engine detects deal closure via webhook
  • Jira project is automatically created with implementation tasks
  • Zendesk customer account is provisioned with support tier
  • ERP system creates customer record and contract
  • Intuit Enterprise generates first invoice
  • Microsoft Teams channel is created for project collaboration
  • All stakeholders receive automated notifications

Business Benefits

  • Reduces onboarding time from 2 days to 15 minutes
  • Eliminates manual data entry errors
  • Ensures consistent customer experience
  • Accelerates time to revenue
  • Provides complete audit trail
Incident Detection and Response
UC-002

Actors

DevOps Team Engineering Team Support Team

Systems Involved

New Relic Prometheus Jira Zendesk Microsoft Teams

Description

When APM systems detect performance degradation or errors, the platform automatically correlates the incident with business impact, creates tickets, notifies teams, and links related customer support issues for faster resolution.

Workflow Steps

  • New Relic detects error rate spike above threshold
  • Prometheus confirms infrastructure metrics anomaly
  • Correlation Engine analyzes business impact
  • Jira incident ticket is automatically created
  • Related Zendesk tickets are linked to incident
  • Microsoft Teams incident channel is created
  • On-call engineer receives alert with full context
  • Executive Dashboard shows real-time customer impact

Business Benefits

  • Reduces mean time to detection (MTTD) by 60%
  • Provides immediate business impact visibility
  • Links technical issues to customer complaints
  • Enables faster root cause analysis
  • Improves customer communication during incidents
Support Ticket to Bug Escalation
UC-003

Actors

Support Agent Engineering Team Product Manager

Systems Involved

Zendesk Jira HubSpot OpenSearch

Description

When support identifies a product bug through customer tickets, the platform automatically creates a Jira bug with full context, links related tickets, and updates the customer on progress.

Workflow Steps

  • Support agent tags Zendesk ticket as "Bug"
  • System searches OpenSearch for similar error patterns
  • Jira bug is created with ticket details and logs
  • Related Zendesk tickets are automatically linked
  • Customer account in HubSpot is flagged for follow-up
  • Engineering team is notified with priority level
  • Zendesk ticket receives automated updates on bug status
  • Customer is notified when fix is deployed

Business Benefits

  • Eliminates duplicate bug reports
  • Provides engineering with complete context
  • Keeps customers informed automatically
  • Tracks customer impact of bugs
  • Improves customer satisfaction scores
Automated Invoice Reconciliation
UC-004

Actors

Finance Team Sales Team Operations Team

Systems Involved

HubSpot ERP Intuit Enterprise Power BI

Description

The platform automatically reconciles invoices across HubSpot deals, ERP orders, and Intuit Enterprise accounting, flagging discrepancies and providing real-time financial visibility.

Workflow Steps

  • ERP system generates order based on HubSpot deal
  • Intuit Enterprise creates invoice from ERP data
  • ETL Pipeline extracts data from all three systems
  • Reconciliation engine compares amounts and details
  • Discrepancies are flagged and assigned to finance team
  • Power BI dashboard shows reconciliation status
  • Automated alerts for overdue payments
  • Monthly financial reports generated automatically

Business Benefits

  • Reduces reconciliation time from days to hours
  • Eliminates manual spreadsheet work
  • Catches billing errors immediately
  • Improves cash flow visibility
  • Ensures financial compliance and auditability
Release Workflow Automation
UC-005

Actors

Engineering Team DevOps Team Product Manager Support Team

Systems Involved

Jira Zendesk Catchpoint New Relic Microsoft Teams

Description

The platform orchestrates the complete release workflow from planning through deployment validation, ensuring all stakeholders are informed and monitoring is in place.

Workflow Steps

  • Jira release is marked as "Ready for Deployment"
  • Workflow Engine validates all tickets are closed
  • Deployment notification sent to all stakeholders
  • Catchpoint synthetic tests run pre-deployment baseline
  • Deployment proceeds with automated rollback capability
  • New Relic monitors error rates and performance
  • Catchpoint validates critical user journeys post-deploy
  • Zendesk knowledge base updated with release notes
  • Support team notified of new features and fixes
  • Executive Dashboard shows deployment health

Business Benefits

  • Reduces deployment risk through automated validation
  • Ensures support team is prepared for customer questions
  • Provides immediate visibility into deployment health
  • Enables faster rollback if issues detected
  • Improves release predictability and quality

User Lifecycle Management
UC-007

Actors

HR Team IT Team Department Managers

Systems Involved

Identity & SSO Microsoft 365 Jira HubSpot Zendesk

Description

The platform automates employee onboarding and offboarding, provisioning and de-provisioning access across all enterprise systems based on role and department.

Workflow Steps

  • HR creates new employee record in identity system
  • Workflow Engine determines required system access by role
  • Microsoft 365 account created with email and Teams
  • Jira account provisioned with appropriate project access
  • HubSpot/Zendesk access granted for sales/support roles
  • Manager receives notification to complete onboarding tasks
  • For offboarding: all access revoked simultaneously
  • Audit log maintained for compliance

Business Benefits

  • Reduces onboarding time from 1 week to 1 hour
  • Ensures consistent access provisioning
  • Improves security through immediate offboarding
  • Maintains compliance with access audit trails
  • Eliminates orphaned accounts
